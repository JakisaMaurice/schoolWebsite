ADMISSIONS

Welcome to Westwood Academy

At Westwood Academy, we are committed to providing a high-quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values to excel in their chosen fields. Our admissions process is designed to be simple and transparent, ensuring that every prospective student has the opportunity to join our vibrant learning community.

Why choose Westwood Academy

  • High-quality education with experienced faculty
  • State-of-the-art learning facilities
  • Practical and hands-on training
  • Supportive and inclusive learning environment
  • Strong industry partnerships for career opportunities

Admissions Process

  • Step 1: Explore Our Programs

    We offer a variety of programs across different disciplines. Browse our courses to find the one that best suits your career goals.

  • Step 2: Check Eligibility Requirements

    Each program has specific entry requirements. Ensure that you meet the academic and other necessary criteria before applying.

  • Step 3: Submit Your Application

    You can apply online or visit our admissions office to complete the application process. Ensure you provide all required documents, including:

    • Academic transcripts and certificates
    • Identification documents (National ID/Passport)
    • Passport-sized photos
    • Any additional program-specific requirements

  • Step 4: Attend an Interview (if required)

    Some programs may require an interview or entrance examination. Our admissions team will notify you if this step is necessary.

  • Step 5: Receive Your Admission Letter

    Successful applicants will receive an official admission letter with details on course registration, fees, and orientation.

  • Step 6: Pay Fees & Enroll

    Complete your fee payment and enrollment process to officially become a student at Westwood Academy.
